    Focus on projects and development approach
    Debating our ideas

    • How we organise the process to get the best results is what we wanted to convey on our site. We wanted to show our production process in an interesting and engaging way. We can use inspiring illustrations to support our story.

    • Ideas take flight through daring design and development.

    design

    We formed layouts from bright and vivid illustrations accompanied by well-structured and easily readable explanations. Texts and illustrations do not overlap so that it’s easier to edit them through CMS. Instead of Photoshop we used Typecast to work on real life web typography.

    illustrations

    The heart of Polecat's first site were Irena's illustrations. Now Irena came up with even more creative and vibrant illustrations for the second version of the site. Illustrations and text content correlate with each other drawing an analogy between airplane and mobile app design and development.
